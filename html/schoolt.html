A Tour of Maria Montessori Preschool just for you:



Our school features fully implemented Montessori environments for our students ages 3 to 6 years old. Because we want to best serve our prospective families, we have established a virtual tour to help you see what makes our center a nurturing home for young learners.
If you aren't ready for an in-person visit we have also established a virtual tour option, please ask us if you would like to know more about this option.

During Your Tour, You'll get to:

Connect one-on-one with the Head of School

Learn about your child's teacher

See your child's classroom

Discover how our school applies Montessori to achieve the best for your child

Get details about your child's day

Ask questions about what's important to you about child's experience here

Admissions



We enroll year-round when there is space available for children in the appropriate classroom and program. If a space is not available for the timeline you are interested in for your child, your application will be considered for the waitlist. There is no fee to join the waitlist.
