Careers & Jobs

Full and Part Time Options Available



We are always interested in speaking with enthusiastic, qualified Montessori educators from Nido through Kindergarten about our open employment opportunities.

With 4 campus locations offering fully implemented Montessori environments, we are always looking for experienced certified Montessori Guides who have a strong background in both classroom management and upper Montessori work.

Offering programs for Nido, Pre-Primary, Primary & Kindergarten, each campus has large spacious classrooms and Studio offerings for Art, Gardening, Cooking and more. This is a wonderful environment for a positive thinking go-getter with the ability to cooperate and lead effectively!

Positions are full-time, year-round with paid Personal Time Off (PTO), school holidays, and paid sick time. We offer health & dental insurance, and very competitive salary.



HOW TO APPLY

Email resume and cover letter to: hr@msosv.com
View all open positions here.

For applications to be considered: All previous employment must be verified and any gaps in employment must be explained with proper documentation.

If you possess a degree in Child Development and have an interest in Montessori Education, or have 12 ECE units, please apply with us. We will provide formal Montessori Education for the right individual. Our salary is competitive, and we are always looking for dynamic, driven staff who wish to become school leaders.
