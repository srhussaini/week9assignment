Kindergarten

As with all of our programs, the Kindergarten offered at our school provides a unique, open environment that emphasizes your child's natural interests and allows your child to set his or her own developmental pace.
The Montessori education journey all began with Dr. Maria Montessori. Through scientific observations, she determined children learn best at their own pace and given the choice of activities.
The practical application of sensory-based and self-directed learning is the most important thread across all our programs including Kindergarten.

Each program builds on the one before it, providing challenges appropriate to both age and skill.

  • Language
  • Mathematics
  • Cultural Studies
  • Spatial learning
  • Self-discovery
  • Leadership
  • Music, and art
