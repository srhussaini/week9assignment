About Us

Curriculum


Our Curriculum is based on the Montessori method, developed by Italian Physician Maria Montessori.

Montessori Method

The Montessori method of education is a system of education for children that seeks to develop natural interests and activities rather than use formal teaching methods. It was developed by physician Maria Montessori.

Preschool

Our Primary Program is for children ages 2½ to 6 years old. Primary Programs generally run from 8:45am until 3:00pm, 5 days a week, with half-days. Children entering a Primary Class should be potty trained.

Kindergarten

As with all of our programs, the Kindergarten offered at our school provides a unique, open environment that emphasizes your child's natural interests and allows your child to set his or her own developmental pace.

“Our care of the child should be governed, not by the desire to make him learn things, but by the endeavor always to keep burning within him that light which is called intelligence.”
- Dr. Maria Montessori

Admissions



We enroll year-round when there is space available for children in the appropriate classroom and program. If a space is not available for the timeline you are interested in for your child, your application will be considered for the waitlist. There is no fee to join the waitlist.
